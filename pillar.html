Pillars of Bangladesh

Innovation

Innovation is the practical implementation of ideas that result in the introduction of new goods or services or improvement in offering goods or services.

Sustainability

Sustainability is a way of using resources in a way that meets the needs of the present without compromising the needs of future generations

Infrastructure

Infrastructure is the basic facilities and systems serving a country, city, or area, such as transportation, power plants, and schools.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the degree of autonomy and self-determination in people and in communities. This enables them to represent their interests in a responsible manner.
